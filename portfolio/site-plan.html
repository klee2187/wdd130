Purpose

The purpose of this site is to create an impressive website to display a gallery of designs I have created. This will allow potential clients or employers an easily navigable and accessible way to see what I am capable of.

My audience is those looking for a web/graphic designer to freelance or join their team. This could include businesses, individuals, or groups of people in need of web or graphic design services.

Our Mission

We’re here to create happiness! Our goal is to bring your ideas to life while sprinkling in a few of our own. We don’t just want you to be satisfied – we want you to be 100% thrilled with the work we deliver. Our mission is to bring smiles not only to our clients but also to their clients when they see our designs in action. We’d love the opportunity to make you smile along the way!

Why Choose Pure Graphix?

At Pure Graphix, we get the job done – and we don’t stop until it’s exactly how you envision it. Our designer pours passion into every project and is dedicated to delivering the perfect design tailored just for you. Design isn’t just about art – it’s about expressing who you are. Whether it's a logo, advertisement, poster, or anything else, our goal is to create something that speaks to you and reflects your unique identity.

About

About the Designer

Welcome!

Hello! My name is Kristen Lee, and I’m a proud nerd with a passion for many fandoms. I’m also a stay-at-home mom to four amazing kids. As an aspiring Graphic and Web Designer, I’m excited to be graduating from Brigham Young University Idaho with a degree in Professional Studies, specializing in Graphic Design and Web Development.

Q & A

Q: What Programs were used to create these designs?

A: The designs in this gallery were created using Adobe programs (Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign), Procreate, and Canva.

Q: How can the designer be contacted?

A: Contact information can be found on the Contact Us page.

Q: What experience does the designer have?

A: Kristen has completed courses at Brigham Young University Idaho and is skilled in a variety of design programs. With nearly 5 years of experience creating .png and .pdf files in Procreate, she has recently expanded her skill set to create .svg files using Adobe Illustrator. Kristen is also very familiar with Cricut Design Space and has designed many items such as t-shirts and hats. In addition to her design expertise, she brings over 12 years of experience in leadership and management as a stay-at-home mom.
